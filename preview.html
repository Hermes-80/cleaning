FAQ

Answer questions before customers hesitate.

FAQ content improves trust and helps visitors understand what happens after they request a quote.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No. We can bring standard supplies and equipment. If you prefer a specific product, fragrance-free option, or your own supplies, note that in your quote request.

Are your products safe for pets and kids?

Pet-friendly and eco-conscious product options are available. Tell us about pets, allergies, sensitivities, or product preferences before your appointment.

What is the difference between standard and deep cleaning?

Standard cleaning maintains an already-kept space. Deep cleaning is more detailed and is better for first-time visits, buildup, baseboards, fixtures, and overlooked areas.

Can I book recurring service?

Yes. Weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, and custom recurring schedules can be quoted based on your home, priorities, and availability.

How quickly will you respond?

During business hours, we aim to reply as quickly as possible. For urgent scheduling, calling or texting 512-486-0448 is the fastest option.
